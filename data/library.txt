The Royal Library

You enter a vast library filled with ancient books and scrolls.
On the main desk, you find a journal written by the castle's architect.

Journal Entry:
"The Golden Key was crafted in the year 1523 by Master Blacksmith Roland.
It consists of THREE parts hidden in different locations:
- Part 1: Hidden in the Secret Garden (see garden.txt)
- Part 2: Locked in the North Tower (see tower.txt)
- Part 3: The final piece location is revealed only when you have clues from both the garden and tower."

The architect's signature: Sir Edmund the Wise
